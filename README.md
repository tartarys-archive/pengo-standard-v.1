# Pengo Standard (v1)

**Pengo** is a cultural and ethical digital standard designed to issue value only through verified physical or intellectual deposits, publicly witnessed by the community.

This repository documents the **Pengo Standard v1**, an open, community-governed framework for culturally grounded digital issuance.

---

## Abstract

Modern digital tokens are often created without origin, memory, or responsibility.

The Pengo Standard proposes a different path.

Pengo jetons are issued **only** when a real-world cultural or intellectual artifact is placed under custodial protection, publicly witnessed on-chain, and acknowledged by the community.

Value does not precede meaning.  
Value follows testimony.

## What Pengo Is Not

- Pengo is **not** a security  
- Pengo is **not** backed by legal ownership  
- Pengo is **not** redeemable for the deposited artifact  
- Pengo is **not** issued arbitrarily  

Pengo is a **signal**, not a contract.

---

## Ethical Foundation

Pengo relies on:
- community witnessing instead of enforcement,
- transparency instead of coercion,
- reputation instead of litigation.

Misuse is addressed through public ethical processes (e.g. Yellow / Black Notice models), not courts.

---

## Governance

The Pengo Standard is maintained under community guardianship within the Tartarys ecosystem.

- No patents are claimed.
- No exclusive ownership exists.
- Revisions occur through documented community consensus.
